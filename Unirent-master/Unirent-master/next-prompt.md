# Redesign UniRent: Premium Academic Marketplace

A high-end, professional redesign of the UniRent student-to-student rental platform.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Light, "Premium Academic"
- Background: Pure Academic White (#FFFFFF)
- Surface: Mist Gray Surface (#F8FAFC)
- Primary Accent: Midnight Scholar Navy (#1B3B5A) for authority and headings
- Secondary Accent: Amber Academy Gold (#D4AF37) for CTAs and highlights
- Text Primary: Near Black (#0F172A)
- Text Secondary: Mist Slate Gray (#64748B)
- Corner Radius: 12px for cards (rounded-xl), 8px for buttons (rounded-lg)
- Shadows: Soft, diffused elevation (whisper-soft)

**Core Pages to Redesign:**

1. **Global Navigation:** Sleek navy navbar, white logo (Stitch/UniRent), right-aligned items: Rentals, Listings, Bookings, Messages, Profile.
2. **Home Landing:**
   - Hero: Modern search bar integrated into a professional academic banner.
   - Values: "Rent Smarter, Study Harder" messaging.
   - Featured Grid: Top listings in high-end card layouts.
3. **Listings (Rentals):** Multi-column grid with professional filters (Category, Price, University). Cards show detailed price/day and owner info.
4. **Auth Pages:** Minimal centered cards on light gray background.

---
💡 **Tip:** This prompt is designed for iterative implementation using Tailwind CSS and React.
