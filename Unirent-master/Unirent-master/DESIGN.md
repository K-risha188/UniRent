# Design System: UniRent (Premium Academic)

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The new UniRent aesthetic is "Premium Academic." It aims to feel trustworthy, sophisticated, and modern—bridging the gap between a student-to-student marketplace and a high-end service platform. The vibe is airy, clean, and professional, using generous whitespace to reduce cognitive load and subtle micro-shadows for tactile depth.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Midnight Scholar Navy (#1B3B5A):** The primary brand color. Used for headings, primary buttons, and navigation backgrounds to convey stability and authority.
* **Amber Academy Gold (#D4AF37):** The accent color. Used for primary calls-to-action (CTAs), highlights, and active states to provide a warm, premium feel.
* **Pure Academic White (#FFFFFF):** The base background color for a crisp, clean canvas.
* **Mist Gray Surface (#F8FAFC):** Used for card backgrounds and subtle section separators to create visual hierarchy.
* **Text Secondary (#64748B):** A muted slate gray for descriptions and metadata.
* **Success Emerald (#10B981):** For positive status indicators like "Available" or "Confirmed."

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** Inter (System Sans-serif) - chosen for its exceptional readability and modern look.
* **Headings:** Bold, Midnight Scholar Navy, slightly increased letter-spacing (-0.01em) for a premium feel.
* **Body Text:** Regular weight, Mist Slate Gray, tracking "normal" for high legibility.
* **Labels:** Medium/Semi-bold, all-caps or small-caps for metadata to differentiate from body text.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:**
    * **Primary:** Midnight Scholar Navy background, White text, 8px rounded corners (rounded-lg), subtle hover elevation.
    * **Secondary:** Amber Academy Gold accent borders or text, transparent or white background.
* **Cards/Containers:**
    * Generously rounded corners (16px / rounded-2xl).
    * Whisper-soft diffused shadows (`shadow-sm` or `shadow-md` with custom opacity) to avoid a "flat" look.
    * White background on Mist Gray surfaces.
* **Inputs/Forms:**
    * Thin borders (1px) in Soft Gray (#E2E8F0), focusing to Midnight Scholar Navy.
    * Subtle internal padding for a "breatheable" feel.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace:** Generous (increments of 12px/24px/48px) to separate functional blocks.
* **Grid:** Standard 12-column grid for desktop, single-column for mobile.
* **Alignment:** Left-aligned headings with centered CTAs in hero sections for maximum impact.
